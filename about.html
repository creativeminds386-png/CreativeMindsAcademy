About Creative Minds Academy

Creative Minds Academy is a CBC-aligned specialist provider offering professional creative and talent-based programs to schools across Kenya. We partner with institutions that want to enrich their learners’ experience without hiring full-time instructors.

Our flexible school-to-school model allows us to deliver high-quality lessons within your timetable, ensuring seamless integration with your existing academic structure.

We bring the specialists. You focus on academics.

Our Mission

To nurture creativity, talent, and personal growth in every learner by delivering high-quality co-curricular programs aligned with the CBC framework.

Our Vision

To be Kenya’s leading provider of specialist co-curricular programs, enabling schools to develop well-rounded, confident, and capable learners.
